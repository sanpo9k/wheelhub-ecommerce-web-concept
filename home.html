The 2024 Toyota GR Corolla

The 2024 Toyota GR Corolla is a high-performance hatchback that combines sporty design with impressive power. Developed by Toyota's Gazoo Racing division, the GR Corolla is designed for enthusiasts who crave an engaging driving experience.

  • Engine: 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-3

  • Transmission: 6-speed manual

2024 Toyota RAV4

The 2024 Toyota RAV4 is a versatile and reliable compact SUV, perfect for a variety of drivers from commuters to adventurers.

  • Engine: 2.5-liter 4-cylinder
  • Plug-in Hybrid: 94 MPGe, 42 miles electric-only range
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic or eCVT (hybrid/plug-in hybrid)

2024 Toyota Prius Prime

The 2024 Toyota Prius Prime is a plug-in hybrid that combines fuel efficiency with the latest in hybrid technology. Known for its eco-friendly performance and modern design, the Prius Prime is an excellent choice for environmentally conscious drivers.

  • Engine: 2.0-liter 4-cylinder hybrid
  • Combined Horsepower: 220 hp
  • Battery: Lithium-ion, 13.6 kWh
