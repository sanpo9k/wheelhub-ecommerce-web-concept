About Wheel Hub

wHeel Hub is a pioneer in automotive retail, providing quality cars and accessories since 1995. Our mission is to redefine the automotive shopping experience through innovation and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Sam Michael, wHeel Hub started as a small family-owned business. With a passion for cars and a commitment to customer satisfaction, wHeel Hub quickly grew into a nationwide brand known for its quality products and personalized service.

Our Mission

At wHeel Hub, our mission is to provide customers with the best automotive shopping experience possible. We strive to offer a wide selection of vehicles, competitive pricing, and top-notch customer service to ensure satisfaction at every step of the journey.

Our Vision

Our vision at wHeel Hub is to be the leading destination for automotive enthusiasts, offering a seamless shopping experience both online and in our showrooms. We aim to continuously innovate and adapt to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
